Welcome to the NUS Education Resource Sharing Website, a comprehensive learning platform specially designed for students and staff of the National University of Singapore. Here, we gather top-quality educational resources from various faculties and disciplines, including course notes, video lectures, academic papers, and practical tools. Whether you need revision materials, research papers, or want to engage in learning discussions, we have it all. This platform enables you to easily share and access the latest educational resources, interact with peers, and enhance your learning efficiency. Join us to create a collaborative learning community where knowledge flows freely, supporting your academic journey every step of the way!

What we offer

Joyful Sharing

At the NUS Educational Resource Sharing Website, we believe that the joy of learning lies in sharing. Whether it’s sharing course notes, study insights, or engaging learning videos, you’ll find like-minded friends here to share the joy of learning with. Let’s create a vibrant and interactive learning community together, making learning a collaborative and enjoyable journey!

Abundant Resources

The NUS Educational Resource Sharing Website boasts a vast collection of high-quality learning resources, including various course materials, academic papers, e-books, and multimedia resources. Whether you need review materials, research references, or new learning content, our website has you covered, helping you enhance your learning efficiency.

Ready to Use

Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and ready to use, ensuring you can start learning and accessing resources without any hassle. Everything you need to excel in your studies is just a click away, making your educational experience seamless and efficient.

Online Payment

Conveniently manage your payments online with our secure payment system, allowing you to focus more on your studies and less on administrative tasks. Our streamlined payment process ensures your transactions are fast, safe, and hassle-free.

Speech to Text

To facilitate your learning, we offer a speech-to-text feature. With this tool, you can easily convert class recordings, lecture content, or any audio material into text files. This not only helps you better organize and review your learning content but also improves your study efficiency and memory retention.

Learning Community

Join our dynamic learning community at NUS Educational Resource Sharing Website. Engage with peers, participate in discussions, and collaborate on projects. Our platform fosters a supportive environment where students can share knowledge, seek help, and grow together.
